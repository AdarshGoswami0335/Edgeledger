Fit Bottomed Girls

“Success, in life, is most easily measured by the number of days you are truly happy.” - Eric Edmeades “Chief Happiness Officer” Tina Su started Think Simple Now back in 2007 as a personal mission to spread happiness, positiveness and compliance in everyone’s lives. Nowadays, her blog is a personal development community enclosing lessons, epiphanies and real life stories of authors from over 200 countries. Their aim is to aspire to find inner clarity and to increase the awareness of what makes life beautiful. The Peace Revolution blog takes its name as homage to the respective organization that strives to bring about world peace through inner peace and to disseminate this positive state of being with people around us. The turning point in this process of sharing good thoughts and mindfulness is ourselves and this why it is important to focus on self-development. Meditation, life wisdom, inner balance and healthy eating mark the beginning of a peace revolution.
